in the beginning

In 2017, the Lord layed it on my heart that sister Esther was to be my wife.

For weeks I was confused and I told myself this must be a joke.

I knew little or nothing about her apart from the fact that we usaully meet at an Ekklesia meeting at Uncles JD's house.

And also, I remember sometimes in 2016, ago that while I was working, cleaning and clearing the surrounding of at white house.

She and sister Abigirl came to help. I believe this was the first time I saw her, and this was the meeting point.

From then on, the next two years for me was full of contempulations, meditations, ponderizations and a quest to gain more understanding about my life and the future and who will the Lord have journey with me.
